About Me

My name is Ida Bagus Gede Prasasta, and I am a graphic designer, 3D artist, and content creator. I often make logo designs for several companies, and I can also make various other 2D designs such as posters, flyers, and photo manipulation. For 3D design, I often make 3D architectural models, various 3D objects for game assets, and 3D illustrations or 3D rendering. I also have basic skills in video editing, animation, and motion graphics. I have earned a bachelor's degree in multimedia at ITB STIKOM Bali. I like to use the time to learn, add to, and improve my skills.
